Welcome to the compliance escape!

The time has come to test your compliance skills... Who is the most compliant employee? Who will survive this ultimate test of proper company policy, and escape the office without setting off a compliance crisis?

In this exam, you and your team will navigate through the maze that is the day-to-day work of a MIcompany analyst. You will fill perform exciting tasks such as filling datalogbooks, locking down offices, and handling data with care. These tasks will be given to you in the form of scenarios, to which you will propose the right course of actions. Based on your actions, you will fight yourself a way through a major project. Points will be awarded both on time, and on how well you answer the questions...
